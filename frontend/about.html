About Us

Islamabad, Pakistan — Est. 2025

Made with intention.

RAAKHSCRN is a screen printing brand born from the belief that everyday objects should carry meaning. Every tote, every print — is a deliberate act against the disposable culture of fast fashion.

We start small. We grow slow. No mass production. No compromises. Each drop is limited, made by hand, and designed to last far longer than a season.

The name RAAKH (راکھ) means "ash" in Urdu — a reminder that beauty can come from what's left behind, what endures, what carries marks of fire.
