From Idea to Ink

The Process

Step by step
01

Concept

Design

Every graphic starts as a hand-drawn concept. Raw lines, deliberate marks. Nothing digital until it absolutely has to be. We sketch on paper, iterate by hand, and let the design breathe before it ever touches a screen.

  • Hand-drawn sketches
  • Concept iteration
  • Final digitization
  • Film positive output
02

Preparation

Screen Burn

The design is exposed onto a mesh screen using UV light and photosensitive emulsion. This locks the design in place — what's exposed hardens, what isn't washes away with water, leaving a perfect stencil in the mesh.

  • Emulsion coating
  • UV exposure
  • Screen washing
  • Final screen check
03

Production

Screen Print

Ink pulled by hand through a screen onto canvas. Each piece gets its own pass — small variations in pressure and angle mean every print is technically unique. We see those variations as features, not flaws. It's the handmade mark.

  • Ink mixing
  • Manual squeegee pull
  • Heat curing
  • Quality inspection
04

Release

The Drop

Limited quantities. No restocks. When a drop sells out, it's gone. We don't chase scale. We build a community of people who care about what they carry. Sign up to know when the next one hits.

  • Limited run only
  • Subscriber first access
  • No restocks
  • Direct WhatsApp order confirmation
